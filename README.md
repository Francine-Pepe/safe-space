## SAFE SPACE

Safe Space is a calm, distraction-free web application designed to help people navigate moments of stress, overwhelm, anxiety, or emotional overload.

Built with Next.js, the app provides simple, accessible tools that encourage users to pause, regulate their emotions, and reconnect with themselves. Rather than focusing on productivity, Safe Space focuses on comfort, emotional well-being, and creating a gentle digital environment that feels safe and supportive.

Current features

* Guided breathing exercises with calming visual cues
* A simple decision flow to help users choose the support they need in the moment
* A private journaling space with automatic saving
* A drawing canvas for expressing thoughts and emotions visually
* PDF export for journaling and drawings, making it easy to share with a therapist or keep for personal reflection
* A clean, low-stimulation interface designed for both desktop and mobile devices
* Accessibility-minded design with support for reduced motion preferences

Planned features

* Sensory regulation tools and ambient sounds
* A personalized comfort box with meaningful content
* Dark mode and additional low-stimulation themes
* Offline support through Progressive Web App (PWA) capabilities
* Gentle daily check-ins and mood tracking

About the project

Safe Space was originally inspired by the idea of creating a supportive digital companion for someone experiencing emotional overwhelm. While the first inspiration came from the needs of a neurodivergent teenager, the goal has grown into building a space that anyone can turn to when life feels too much.

Whether someone needs a moment to breathe, write, draw, or simply pause, Safe Space aims to offer a calm and welcoming place without judgment or pressure.
